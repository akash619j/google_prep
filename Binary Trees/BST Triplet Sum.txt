//find a triplet sum is equal to 0 or not in a bst 


SOLN 1-

Consider every triplet and check whether the 
required sum is zero or not.

Time Compleixty: O(n^3)
Auxillary Space: O(1)


SOLN 2-

Do the inorder traversal of the given bst and
use it to find a triplet has a sum equal to
zero or not.

Time Compleixty: O(n^2)
Auxillary Space: O(n)


SOLN 3-

Convert the bst to a DLL and use it to  find
a triplet with sum equla to zero.

Read it later.

Time Compleixty: O(n^2)
Auxillary Space: O(logn)
